About Me

I am a third-year undergraduate student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science with an Extended Major in Artificial Intelligence.

My research interests lie in Large Language Models (LLMs), including LLM decision-making, uncertainty quantification, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems. Currently, I am interested in exploring related topics in agentic AI.

Currently, I am on a semester exchange at The University of Texas at Austin, where I am taking advanced courses in Software Engineering and Machine Learning for Edge AI.
